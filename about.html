About me

I obtained my diploma as a Front-End Developer from Buroscope (Rennes, France) in 2016, I then started working as a self employed developer and web marketing consultant. This includes designing and coding, and SEO and SEA strategies.

With several friends, I launched the Scan Eat app in 2017, for which I was Project Manager for two years. The app now has over 500,000 downloads.
I then worked as a marketing expert for Facebook. My job was to help a portfolio of customers optimizing their campaigns on the platform. While holding these positions, I continued my freelancing activities.

As a developper

Being partly self-taught, I think I can say that I am resourceful, determined and passionate. I believe that a developer cannot stop learning at any point in his career, and that's good, because I am always looking for the next thing to discover.

As a colleague

I am a team player, I believe in collective intelligence, the ability of a group to overcome obstacles. Having said that, I also believe in the capacity of each individual to surpass themselves and to learn from their own mistakes.

As a person

I have had many jobs in my life, in many different fields and I have learned a lot from them. I know the value of hard work, dedication and commitment. As mentioned earlier, I am naturally very curious, I love to learn new things. My hobbies are diverse, ranging from video games (mostly indie), to reading, watching movies to the NBA. I often listen to music while coding.
