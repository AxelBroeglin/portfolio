Project purpose and goal

This project allows user to determine how many minutes of practicing the activity of his choice it will take to burn the amount of calories contained in 100 grams of the food of his choice. This project is a good summary of concepts often encountered in JavaScript, and allows me to show my knowledge and abilities.

Web Stack choice

I chose the usual trinity of HTML, CSS and JavaScript for their efficiency. I left Sass aside during the development of the app, the design being very simple.
