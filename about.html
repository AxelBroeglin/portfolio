About me

I obtained my diploma as a Technical Integrator (online PDF, in French) in 2016, I then started working as a self employed developer and web marketing consultant. I helped several companies and individuals to create their websites, and I developed web marketing strategies (SEO, SEA) for several clients.

With several friends, I launched the Scan Eat app in 2017, I worked as a project manager for two years. The app now has over 500,000 downloads, only thanks to word of mouth, we are proud of it!

I then worked as a marketing expert for Facebook, while continuing to build several websites and run several SEA campaigns (particularly using Adwords) for different customers.
